# 07 — Phase Plan & Software Development Plan

> **What this is**: A **low-precision** estimate of the elaboration phase's
> duration and effort, plus the tools, people, and resources needed.
> (Larman, *Applying UML and Patterns*, Ch. 4 §4.3, Table 4.1.)
>
> **Important**: Larman warns explicitly (Ch. 4 §4.1) that estimates and
> plans during inception are **not reliable**. They give an order-of-magnitude
> sense of effort, nothing more. Reliable estimates come during elaboration
> after some real programming and testing has surfaced the unknowns.
>
> If you're feeling pressure to produce a precise plan in inception, push
> back. The whole point of iterative development is that you don't pretend
> to know things you don't yet know.
