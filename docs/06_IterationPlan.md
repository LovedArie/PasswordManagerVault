# 06 — Iteration Plan

> **What this is**: What you'll do in the **next** iteration — not the whole
> project. Per Larman (*Applying UML and Patterns*, Ch. 4 §4.3, Table 4.1),
> the Iteration Plan is short and focused on the immediate next step.
>
> **Iteration length**: Larman recommends iterations of about 2–6 weeks
> (Ch. 2). Don't go longer than that.
>
> **Ranking criteria** (Ch. 8 §8.3): rank work by **risk**, **coverage**, and
> **criticality** (business value). Earliest iterations should attack the
> riskiest, most architecturally significant items.
